I saw this post from the Web Design Museum captioned 'The golden age of web design'.

It made me think of the fusion between games and digital design, there's an energy and a coherent form. The internet arrived and provided a path forwards, code provided the means for a new aesthetic. These websites had hope in them for something new and exciting. Websites with sound effects, haptics, movement and ambient sound.
