---
title: "Where's the slime?"
date: 2022-10-15T12:03:00+07:00
---
Thought experiment - what if visual designs, components and websites weren’t clean with gradients, fluid animations, pixel perfect serif fonts? What if they were dirty, yucky, grew within your viewport like fungus? Images and text decayed over time. Link rot was encouraged. Sections that were separate gradually meld together to create new forms.

Not the wisest idea for client work but I might start researching if any one else is exploring this area.
