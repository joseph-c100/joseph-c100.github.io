Speculative homeware brand - 'You Do You' Launch

'This small independent homeware company is dedicated to creating high quality and sustainable products that reflect the tastes and styles of local creatives. Operating out of their retail shop in London, they believe in supporting local artisans and makers while promoting sustainability in their production methods. 

With a variety of items ranging from furniture to decor, all of their products are designed with a special focus on craftsmanship.'
