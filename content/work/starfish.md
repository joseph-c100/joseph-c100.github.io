Self-published zine - 2021

Starfish Press began as a continuation to the publication of 'Starfish' in 2021.

Still in the process of being established, the aim of Starfish is to create visual work which encounters new ideas that bridge print and digital culture.

Co-editor and photographer - [Annie Joy Boothroyd](https://www.annieboothroyd.com)
Layout and writing - Joseph C
